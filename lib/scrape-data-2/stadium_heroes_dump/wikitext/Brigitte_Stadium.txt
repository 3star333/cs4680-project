{{PatchTableStart}}<onlyinclude>
{{PatchTableElement|2025-10-14|
{{DevComment|Developer Comments: Brigitte's Weapon Power builds have struggled to find their footing. We're increasing the usability on these powers to help make Weapon Power centric builds a more viable option.}}

{{al|Burst Aid}} - Power
* Input changed to Reload (from Quick Melee).
* Max Life Heal per Repair Pack consumed increased to 25% (up from 20%).
* Attack Speed Buff per Repair Pack consumed increased to 10% (up from 5%).

{{al|Righteous Cleave}} - Power
* Heal now triggers on next Damaging Rocket Flail Swing (changed from Next Rocket Flail Swing).
}}
{{PatchTableElement|2025-10-01|
{{DevComment|Developer Comments: Brigitte's damage buff to Shield Bash in the core game in the midseason proved to be a large unintended damage buff for Sköldkastning and it has been overperforming. We're bringing down the bonus damage to be more in line with her other powers to ensure variety in her build paths.}}

{{al|Sköldkastning}} - Power
* Shield Boomerang damage reduced to 40% of Shield Bash damage (down from 60%).
}}

{{PatchTableElement|2025-09-04|
{{DevComment|Developer Comments: Scavenged Steel was previously allowing players to chain Rallies together, making it feel like she had Rally every fight. This change should lower Rally uptime so there are greater windows of opportunity to counterplay. We're also lightly increasing the healing reliability of Brigitte's Weapon Power build.}}

{{al|Righteous Cleave}} - Power
* Healing Range increased to 10m (Up from 5m).

{{al|Scavenged Steel}} - Epic Ability Hero Item
