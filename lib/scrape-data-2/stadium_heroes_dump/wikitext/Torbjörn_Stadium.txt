== Balance Change Log ==
{{PatchTableStart}}<onlyinclude>
{{PatchTableElement|2025-10-14|
* Added to the Stadium Hero Roster.

{{al|All Grown Up}} - Power
* While Overload is active, Turrets are upgraded 1 Level to a maximum of Level 3.

{{al|Turriplets}} - Power
* You can have 3 Turrets active. They start at Level 1, have 50% Health and 66% Attack Speed, and can be upgraded temporarily by hitting it with Forge Hammer up to 15s.

{{al|Positive Reinforcement}} - Power
* When a Turret is placed, it gains 200 Overhealth for 5s. Hitting a Turret with Forge Hammer grants you 10 Overhealth for 5s, up to 50.

{{al|Magmini}} - Power
* After using Overload, gain 2 Ammo of Molten Core with 66% reduced damage duration.

{{al|Swedish Sauna}} - Power

{{al|Come Get Yer Armor}} - Power
* After using Overload, throw 2 Armor Packs that grants Armor equal to 20% of your Max Life. Armor Packs do not stack.

{{al|Dwarlord}} - Power
* Overload grants all allies within 5m Overhealth equal to 50% of Overload Overhealth and 10% Attack Speed.

{{al|Let Off Some Steam}} - Power
* While Overload is not active, Weapon Damage and Weapon Healing reduces Overload Cooldown by 10%.

{{al|Riveting}} - Power
* Every 5th Rivet Gun shot shoots both your Primary Fire and Secondary Fire. The bonus shot deals 25% reduced damage.

{{al|Clocked}} - Power

{{al|Forged in Fire}} - Power

{{al|Blacksmith}} - Power
